EYE CARE

Eyes are most sensitive organ of our body. Any kind of carelessness can ruin the perfect vision. At Eye Care we take utmost care to give your eyes the best treatment they deserve. Eye Care is a centre equipped with all ultramodern facilities for eye checkup and cataract operations. Our motto is to provide quality eye services at affordable cost and hence eye care at its best.

EQUIPMENTS

Eye Care is equipped with State of Art ultra modern facilities viz:
  • Compact Phacoemulsification system from Abott Medical Optics (USA) with advanced ICE & CASE Technology for safe and secure Cataract surgery
  • Carl Zeiss Operating Microscope ( Germany) for better visualization of eye anatomy during surgery.
  • Topcon Autorefracto Keratometer for accurate and reliable assessment of refractive error and K readings.
  • Appasamy Slit Lamp with Haagstreit (Germany) Goldman Applanation Tonometer.
  • Tomey Digital Lensometer to verify your glasses number.
  • Tomey Pachymeter for assessing corneal thickness helpful in glaucoma and Lasik treatment.
  • Sonomed A-Scan for calculating accurate IOL power
  • Humphrey field analyser for glaucoma diagnosis and management.
  • Digital Fundus Fluorescein Angiography for management of diabetic retinopathy and other diseases.
  • Nidek Green Laser for treatment of various retinal diseases.
  • Synaptophore and CAM Stimulator for eye muscle exercises.
  • Heinz Indirect Ophthalmoscope, Direct Ophthalmoscope and many other stereoscopic lenses.
